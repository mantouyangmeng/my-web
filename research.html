Gut Microbiota & m6A Epitranscriptomic Regulation

Investigating how antibiotic-induced gut microbiota dysbiosis reprograms the host transcriptome and m6A epitranscriptome through bile acid metabolism, revealing molecular mechanisms of microbe–host interactions.

Representative publication: Advanced Science, 2024

Neural Metabolism & Vitamin Regulation

Exploring how nutrients such as folate and vitamins regulate neural mRNA m6A methylation through carbon metabolism pathways in both Drosophila and mammalian models, uncovering post-transcriptional regulatory mechanisms.

Representative publications: Communications Biology 2025; Genome Research 2026

Disease Models & Multi-omics Integration

Integrating transcriptomics and metabolomics in Parkinson's, Alzheimer's, and dengue models to analyze interactive effects of environmental factors (e.g., blue light exposure) and aging.

Representative publications: Microbiology Spectrum 2023; PNAS Nexus 2023; Advanced Science 2026
