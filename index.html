About Me

I study the intersection of innate immunity, gut microbiota, and sleep rhythms, integrating m6A epitranscriptomics with multi-omics analyses (transcriptomics, proteomics, metabolomics, and 16S) in Drosophila and mouse models. Self-taught in bioinformatics (R / Perl).
